About

I'm a student of Mathematical Physics at the University of Waterloo! What does that tell you about me? It means that I am obsessed with math, physics, and the basic sciences in general.

One of my favourite things is education - providing new opportunities for students to learn things that they wouldn't have heard of otherwise. This is why I've been involved with the Physics Club for my entire degree. Building a physics community is the key to allowing students to communicate and grow, and therefore allows them to experience more things than they would have just sitting in their dorm.

For the past two years I've been involved with my university's iGEM team, which is a student research team which develops new applications of synthetic biology, which is the application of genetics and molecular biology to engineering.
